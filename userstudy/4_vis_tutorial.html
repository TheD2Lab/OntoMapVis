Visualization Tutorial

Visualization support

While evaluating a given set of mappings, you will be assisted with visualizations of the ontologies that are being mapped.
The visualizations are designed to help you interact with the classes in the ontologies in order to determine whether the given mappings are correct and complete.
You will be shown video tutorials of the visualization that will be used in your task.

What to expect?
