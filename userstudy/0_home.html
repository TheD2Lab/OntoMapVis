This study investigates how two types of visualization support the user during ontology mapping evaluation tasks. No prior knowledge of ontology or ontology mapping is required, as you will be shown short tutorials on these subjects before carrying out any task.
In this study, you will be asked to:

  • consent your participation
  • follow a short tutorial on ontology, ontology mapping and two types of visualization
  • evaluate some mapping results assisted by the visualizations shown in the tutorial
  • complete questionnaires and express your experience with the tasks and the visualizations
