Visualizatoin Study - A

This study investigates how a type of visualizations supports the user during ontology mapping evaluation tasks.
No prior knowledge of ontology or ontology mapping is required, as you will be shown short tutorials on these subjects before carrying out any task.

In this study, you will be asked to:

  • follow a short tutorial task and an interactive visualization
  • evaluate some mappings with the visualization support shown in tutorial
  • take a short survey on your experience
